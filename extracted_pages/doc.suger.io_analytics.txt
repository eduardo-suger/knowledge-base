Introduction
Suger Analytics is a powerful, cloud-based data analytics platform designed to help businesses import, analyze, and visualize data with ease.  By integrating effortlessly with the Suger platform, it provides businesses with the ability to access and analyze data from various sources, including marketplace and cosell data, for deeper, actionable insights.
