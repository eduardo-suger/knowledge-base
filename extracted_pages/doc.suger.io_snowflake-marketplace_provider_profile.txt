Provider Profile
Before listing your product on Snowflake Marketplace, you need to set up your provider profile.
Overview
​
A provider profile represents your company on the Snowflake Marketplace. It contains essential information about your organization and is required before you can list any data products or applications.

Managing Your Profile
​
Creating/Editing a Profile
​
Sign in to Snowsight
Switch to ACCOUNTADMIN role or a role with CREATE DATA EXCHANGE LISTING privilege
Navigate to Data Products » Provider Studio » Profiles
Select Update Profile (or create new)
Fill in required information
Submit for approval
Note:
Most profile changes require Snowflake approval before becoming visible in the Marketplace, except for Business and Technical contact updates.
Deleting a Profile
​
You can delete a provider profile if it's not associated with any listings:
Sign in to Snowsight
Switch to ACCOUNTADMIN role or appropriate privileged role
Navigate to Data Products » Provider Studio » Profiles
Select the profile
Choose Delete Profile
Confirm deletion
Important:
Ensure no listings are associated with the profile before attempting deletion.
