Save yourself the work of writing custom integrations for QuickBooks by connecting Suger to your QuickBooks.
Create Integration

Go to
Settings
and set up
Redirect URIs
in your QuickBooks App, it should be
https://api.suger.cloud/public/integration/quickbooks/oauthCallback
Find
Client ID
&
Client secret
in
Keys & credentials
. You'll need them in step 4
Click the "Connect" button on the "Integrations" page.
Fill in your credentials in the popup
QuickBooks's authorization page will open up in a new tab, asking you to login (if you haven't yet), and select an account to start the integration.
The integration status will become "Connected". Now locate the QuickBooks integration in the list, and click "Verify".
Suger will attempt to read a few opportunities to verify the connection, after which the "Status" will become "Verified".
Now you are all set!
