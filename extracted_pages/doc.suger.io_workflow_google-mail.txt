Google Mail
Send emails using Gmail.
The Google Mail node allows you to automate email operations in Gmail. Before using this node, you'll need to connect your Google Mail account to Suger. See
Google Mail Integration
for more details.
Operations
​
Send Email
​
The
Send Email
operation lets you send emails programmatically. Here are the available fields:
To
: Recipient email address(es)
Subject
: Email subject line
Body
: Main content of the email
CC
: Carbon copy recipients
Example Configuration
​
Tips
​
Multiple email addresses in To, CC fields should be comma-separated
The Body field supports both plain text and HTML formatting
